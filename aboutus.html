Glenn's work and life experience are noteworthy. Growing up in a broken home, Glenn understood the importance of hard work and effort. He became actively involved in community endeavors and put himself through college, earning a BA in Political Science from Eastern Illinois University in Charleston, Illinois. Glenn's career in real estate began at the age of twenty-five and includes successful stints in government and commercial assets. Glenn believes people are the most important asset any organization has. His proudest accomplishment is mentoring nine Assistant Property Managers who were colleagues to Property/General Managers. Glenn believes education is one of the keys to success. In addition to his formal education, he has earned his BOMA RPA, FMA, and BOMI-HP certifications. Additional certifications include CPM, LEED Green Associate, and California Real Estate Broker.

Glenn believes giving back to the community is an obligation that must be embraced. He is the immediate Past President of BOMA San Francisco, President of BOMA Pacific Southwest Region, Co-Chairperson of BOMA International Workforce Advocacy Committee, a member of the BOMA International's Finance Committee, and Chairperson of the Audit Committee. Additionally, Glenn is the Secretary of BOMA California and a member of the California State Historical Safety Board. Glenn is also a founding member of the Downtown SF Partnership organization and currently serves as Vice President

Glenn's passion for serving and helping others is demonstrated by his volunteer efforts on behalf of AIDS Walk San Francisco. In 2023, Glenn raised $60,350, placing him #1 among the walkers. In 2023, BOMA International Fellows recognized Glenn with the organization's highest honor. The honor reflects exemplary and sustained contributions to the real estate industry, property management profession, the community, and BOMA at all levels.
Glenn lives in San Francisco, is a marathon runner, enjoys making homemade pastries, and loves to travel.
